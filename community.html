Why Is This Community Interesting?

Languages In Contact

Our project examines the only community in the world in which Spanish and Afrikaans have coexisted over multiple generations.


Endangered Language & Cultural Variety

Our work is timely as the shifts from Afrikaans to Spanish and from Calvinism to Roman Catholicism are advancing so rapidly that the current oldest generation represents the last group of fluent bilingual speakers and includes some of the last members of the Dutch Reformed Church in Argentina.


Fusion of Research Methodologies

The Collaboratory affords our team the opportunity to test how linguistic and cultural research traditions can both inform and challenge each other.
